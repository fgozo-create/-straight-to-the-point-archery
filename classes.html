Our Classes

Explore our range of archery classes designed for beginners, intermediate learners, and advanced archers.

A101 Basic Archery

This beginner-level course focuses on archery safety, proper form, and basic shooting techniques. Fun activities are included to help build confidence and skill.

  • Schedule: Mondays & Wednesdays (4–5 PM)
  • Cost: $120/month
  • Prerequisite: None (Minimum age 8)

A102 Intermediate Archery

This course builds on fundamental skills and introduces new equipment such as recurve and compound bows. Focus is placed on improving form and consistency.

  • Schedule: Tuesdays & Thursdays (4–5 PM)
  • Cost: $120/month
  • Prerequisite: A101 or instructor approval

A103 Advanced Archery

Designed for experienced students, this course focuses on tournament-style shooting and advanced techniques. Students receive personalized guidance to refine their performance.

  • Schedule: Tuesdays & Thursdays (5–6 PM)
  • Cost: $120/month
  • Prerequisite: A101 & A102 or equivalent
